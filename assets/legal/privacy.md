# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 28, 2026

**Odyssey** ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Pranta Dutta. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Odyssey mobile application ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile picture (if provided)

### Authentication Data
- Login credentials (securely hashed)
- Google Sign-In tokens (if using Google authentication)
- Firebase authentication identifiers

### Trip & Travel Data
- Trip itineraries, destinations, and dates
- Activities, packing lists, and budgets
- Travel notes and journal entries

### Media
- Photos and videos you upload to your trips
- Memory pins and associated location data

### Location Information
- Location data associated with trip activities and memory pins
- Map interaction data (viewed regions, saved places)

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Push notification tokens

### Purchase Data
- Subscription status and type
- In-app purchase history (processed through Apple App Store / Google Play Store)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
- Enable trip planning, tracking, and memory storage
- Sync your data across devices
- Send push notifications (trip reminders, sharing invitations)
- Process subscriptions and in-app purchases
- Generate travel statistics and achievements
- Improve the App's performance and features

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored securely on our servers using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Sensitive credentials (passwords, tokens) are encrypted at rest
- The App supports offline functionality; local data is stored on your device using encrypted local storage
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following cases:

- **With other users:** When you explicitly share trips or accept collaboration invitations
- **Service providers:** Firebase (authentication, push notifications), cloud hosting providers
- **Legal requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

- **Firebase** (Google) - Authentication, push notifications, and analytics
- **Google Sign-In** - Optional social login
- **Apple App Store / Google Play Store** - In-app purchases and subscriptions

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data stored in the App
- **Update** your profile information at any time
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your trip data
- **Opt out** of push notifications through device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

---

## 7. Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
- Anonymous, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.
